8 DEMANDS THAT WOULD DECREASE POLICE VIOLENCE
8cantwait calls for cities to adopt 8 common sense police policies, like banning chokeholds, strangleholds and shooting at moving vehicles and requiring police officers to issue a warning before shooting and make comprehensive reports on every use of force. But none of these demands strike at the fundamental problem: that police forces continue to exist and terrorize our neighborhoods. A lot of their reforms have already been implemented across major cities in the United States, yet police violence continues unabated.

Here's a list of 8 more demands that are centered around abolition.
EXPEL POLICE UNIONS
THE AFL-CIO NEEDS TO EXPEL & BREAK POLICE UNIONS
This all has to start with the AFL-CIO expelling police unions and then ultimately breaking them. No union contract/no collective bargaining. We saw how effective that was with Scott Walker & the teachers unions in 2010 and we honestly can't be coy about using the same tactics. Everything that follows will be undermined in 1-5 years if that's not step one.

Community oversight boards don’t work while you’ve got the existing police unions. You can see this with the way the Rochester, NY Police Union undermined the Police Accountability Board to make it utterly toothless - it can make recommendations but it can't actually hold police accountable.
